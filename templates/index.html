About Aushadhi
An open, free medicine information tool built for Indian patients and caregivers — covering generic drugs and popular Indian brand names.
Drug lookup
Search by generic name or Indian brand name. Get dosage, uses, side effects and precautions in plain language.
Interaction checker
Check if two medicines are safe to take together. Severity rated low, moderate or high with safer alternatives suggested.
Personal profile
Save your current medicines, allergies and conditions. Data stays on your device — nothing is stored externally.
Privacy first
No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Everything runs in your browser. Open source and free forever.
